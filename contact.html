Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for an event?

Navigate to the "Register" page, fill out the form with your details, and select the event you wish to attend from the dropdown list.

Is there a fee for registering?

Some events are completely free, while premium events may require a registration fee. The details are provided on the specific event page.

Can I cancel my registration?

Yes, you can cancel your registration up to 48 hours before the event starts by contacting our support team.
